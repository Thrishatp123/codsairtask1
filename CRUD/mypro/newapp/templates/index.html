About Nutribbles

Being stuck in a corporate loop, life was mundane and colourless. Our
founders Rinu and Dany were looking for ways to break out of it.
Thankfully they did not have to go very far. The answer was right in the
backyard of their house in a beautiful town in Wayanad, Kerala in the
form of a ‘Chakka’ aka Jackfruit on the 50 year old ‘Plaavu’ aka
Jackfruit tree.
Having heard of the benefits of Jackfruit as part of daily meals, they
gathered all the information they could on the possibility and
goodness of Jackfruit powder. A training/workshop on behalf of the
Panchayat/Government, lots of research on Internet and more
homework brought them to the conclusion that ‘Chakka Podi’ or
‘Jackfruit Powder’ could be the solution to their mundane lives. As the
next step they identified Jackfruit sources, possible markets, steps to
get the license in India and other countries and export methods.

Our Process

  • Sources

    Estates in wayanad which could provide them with bulk produce was their first find. Through the Panchayat they met the Kudumbasree units who helped them source locally and clean and pick the jackfruit to processable stage.

  • Processing

    Next came the processing stage, which was just 2 simple stepsDehydrating the jackfruit using dryers Milling them to the final product

  • Packing

    The product was tested in different authorised laboratories for various certifications before packaging to the current format.
